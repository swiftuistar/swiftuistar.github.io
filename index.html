Suvega

Design vector graphics / logos / icons on your mobile device, with visualization tools and number pickers.

  • Export SVG
  • Built-in rounded rectangle / regular polygon / ellipse
  • Fill or stroke with colors and linear gradients
  • Multi-layer support
  • Create custom shapes with lines and curves
  • Rotate, scale and translate shapes
